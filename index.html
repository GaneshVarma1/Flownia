Frequently Asked Questions

  • Who can use your software?

    Business owners with multiple businesses can use the software to access all business details and accounts in one place, including convenience stores, gas stations, bulk fuel storage facilities, LPG, CNG, LNG, blunt, and ethanol storage facilities.

  • What type of devices do we need?

    Our software only requires an internet connection and can work on smartphones, tablets, laptops, and desktops. It's designed to help you manage your business accounts easily. Our cloud-based software allows you to securely log in to your portal using web browsers compatible with Android and iOS platforms. There's no need for open-source software to access it.

  • How much time it will take to implement the software in our business?

    In just 10 minutes, we'll craft a bespoke dashboard for your business, ready to fuel your success. Harness its power and watch your business soar.

  • How much time will it take to understand the software?

    Our dashboard is intuitively designed, backed by an online training sandbox for hands-on learning. Dive into short, informative videos and help documents for every step, ensuring you're never stuck. Need assistance? Reach out to us at 518-992-2580 for expert support.

  • How do I know my data is secure ?

    We implement a robust AAA data security policy, combining blockchain credit technology with a HIPAA security layer for stringent user controls. Our vertical Goth monitoring system oversees user access, Jio locations, and automatic logging, backed by daily periodic backups. All access points adhere to audit controls and feature two-factor authentication for enhanced security measures.

  • Is the Service available 24 x 7?

    Absolutely, we're here for you round the clock, ensuring 99% uptime with a rock-solid SLA. That remaining 1% is reserved for essential system maintenance, upgrades, and seamless deployments. For further details, feel free to reach out to us anytime.
